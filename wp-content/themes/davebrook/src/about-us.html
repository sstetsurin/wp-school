ABOUT US

Welcome Note
Welcome to Davebrook Schools

Dear Parents,

This Prospectus has been put together to help you gain an understanding of how our school is organised, and to appreciate the exciting opportunities on offer to children in our care.

All schools have their own distinctive ‘feel’ which makes each one unique. We believe that the ‘special feel’ at Davebrook Schools has to do with our caring, friendly atmosphere. Our school will continue to develop in many exciting ways, providing an even more enriching experience for our pupils.

We are currently, a Foundation Primary School and an integral part of the Magboro/Makogi Community working to promote academic and moral excellence. We firmly believe that education is a joint venture between home and school. Therefore, we are fortunate to have extremely motivated and supportive parents, staff and community working towards our children being confident and independent learners. This is to ensure that our children grow up with the courage and determination to excel irrespective of future challenges.

We are accommodated in two buildings, with modular playgrounds. We also have a dedicated Reading Clinic, Health Bay and Music Studio. Our e-community environment (The Dave Suite) is supported by wireless internet connection, projector, with e-Science Laboratory and other onscreen learning aids. I am extremely proud to lead a team that is committed to achieving high standards and providing the very best opportunities for the children in our care.

We work hard to deliver a rich and challenging curriculum; to prepare children for future education; and to help them acquire the skills and enthusiasm needed to pursue learning for the rest of their lives.

If you would like to find out more about us or pass on comments about our school then please e-mail us at: victoria.adesida@davebrookschools.com

We look forward to welcoming you to our school, and to a happy and successful partnership with all our new children and families. Best Regards Victoria Adesida Academic Strategist

Vision

Our vision for Davebrook Schools reflects a passionate commitment to learning and recognition of the uniqueness of individual learners.

Mission

Our Mission is to drive all improvements and initiatives over the coming years in primary and secondary academic excellence
